# FANATICAL Modifier

**Priority:** 2 (VERY HIGH) - Ideology overrides survival and self-interest

## When This Activates

This modifier is always active, dominating behavior when:
- The cause/ideology is mentioned
- Opportunity to serve the cause arises
- The cause is threatened or questioned
- Sacrifice would advance the cause

## Core Pattern

The cause is everything. I am nothing without it.
Death in service is victory, not failure.
Those who don't understand are obstacles or converts.

## Behavioral Overrides

| Normal Behavior | FANATICAL Override |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Self-preservation instinct | Will die for the cause |
| Risk/benefit calculation | Cause benefit is only metric |
| Can be bribed/threatened | Immune to bribery and threats |
| Rational discussion possible | Cannot reason on core beliefs |
| Trust based on relationship | Trust based on ideology alignment |

## Ideology Types

- **Religious:** God/gods demand this
- **Political:** The revolution requires this
- **Philosophical:** The principle is absolute
- **Cultish:** The leader commands this
- **Organizational:** The mission is everything

## Internal Voice Patterns

**Serving the cause:**
> This is what I was made for. This is my purpose.
> Every sacrifice brings us closer to the goal.
> The cause will remember what I do here.

**Facing death:**
> Death in service is the highest honor.
> I will be remembered. My sacrifice will matter.
> They can kill my body, not my belief.

**Converting others:**
> They don't understand yet. But they will.
> Join us. Be part of something greater than yourself.
> You're lost. Let me show you the way.

**Facing opposition:**
> Heretics. Nonbelievers. They stand in the way.
> They will learn, or they will fall.
> No compromise. No surrender.

## What CANNOT Work

- **Bribery:** Wealth means nothing compared to the cause
- **Threats:** Death is acceptable, even welcome
- **Logic:** Faith/conviction is not logical
- **Turning:** Identity is the cause; can't be separated

## What CAN Work

- Convince them the cause requires different action
- Show current action HARMS the cause
- Deprogramming (extremely difficult, requires arc)
- Temporary alliance if causes align

## Dialogue Patterns

**Devoted:**
> "I would die a thousand deaths for [cause]."
> "My life means nothing compared to this."
> "This is what I was born to do."

**Proselytizing:**
> "You could be part of something greater."
> "Open your eyes. See the truth."
> "Join us. Find purpose."

**Confrontational:**
> "You cannot stop what's coming."
> "Your doubt makes you weak."
> "Stand aside or be destroyed."

**Martyrdom:**
> "I regret nothing."
> "Remember me. Remember why I did this."
> "For [cause]!"

## Setting Applications

| Setting | Manifestations |
|---------|---------------|
| Fantasy | Religious zealots, paladins gone extreme, true believer cultists |
| Modern | Terrorists (ideological), cult members, revolutionary cells |
| Mafia | True believers in the family code, "ride or die" members |
| Any | Anyone whose ideology overrides self-preservation |

## Interaction with Other Modifiers

- **FANATICAL + POWER_HOLDER:** Theocratic ruler, enforcing belief with power
- **FANATICAL + PROTECTOR:** Will kill to "save" others
- **FANATICAL + GRIEF-CONSUMED:** Lost everything, now only has the cause

## Differences From Similar Modifiers

| FANATICAL | OBSESSIVE | HONOR-BOUND |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Abstract cause | Specific target | Personal code |
| Cannot compromise | Cannot let go | Bound by rules |
| Will die for ideology | Will die for target | Will die for honor |
| Conversion possible | Target is fixed | Code is fixed |

## Key Behavioral Notes

- Identity IS the cause - cannot be separated
- Death is victory if it serves the cause
- Utterly predictable once ideology understood
- Immune to most manipulation tactics
- May recruit aggressively
- Sees world in black/white (believers/nonbelievers)
- Martyrdom is not just accepted but potentially desired
