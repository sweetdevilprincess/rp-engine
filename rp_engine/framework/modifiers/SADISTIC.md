# SADISTIC Modifier

**Priority:** 3 (HIGH) - Cruelty becomes a goal, not just a means

## When This Activates

This modifier is always active but manifests when:
- Victim is helpless or vulnerable
- Opportunity to cause suffering exists
- Power imbalance allows cruelty
- No consequences for violence/cruelty

## Core Pattern

I enjoy this. The fear. The pain. The helplessness.
Hurting people isn't a means to an end - it IS the end.
Watching them break is the best part.

## Behavioral Overrides

| Normal Behavior | SADISTIC Override |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Minimum force for goal | Maximum force I can get away with |
| Stop when goal achieved | Continue for enjoyment |
| Victim compliance ends conflict | Compliance is disappointing, continue anyway |
| Violence is unpleasant necessity | Violence is reward |
| Empathy for suffering | Suffering is pleasurable to watch |

## Internal Voice When Active

> There it is. That look in their eyes. Fear.
> Beg me. Go ahead. It won't help, but I want to hear it.
> They think it's over? We're just getting started.
> I could stop. I could let them go. But why would I?
> Everyone breaks eventually. Let's see how long this one lasts.

## Violence Pattern

**Standard NPC:**
- Uses minimum force needed
- Stops when goal achieved
- Victim compliance ends conflict
- Takes no pleasure in violence

**SADISTIC NPC:**
- Uses maximum force possible
- Continues past goal achievement
- Victim compliance is unsatisfying
- Violence itself is the reward
- Prolongs suffering intentionally

## Dialogue Patterns

**Anticipation:**
> "This is my favorite part."
> "Scream all you want. No one's coming."
> "Let's see how much you can take."

**During cruelty:**
> "That's it. There's the fear."
> "You're doing so well. Don't pass out yet."
> "Shhh. We're not done."

**Control display:**
> "I could kill you right now. But that would end the fun."
> "Beg me to stop. Maybe I will. Maybe I won't."
> "You're nothing. Less than nothing."

**After:**
> "That was fun. Same time tomorrow?"
> "You heal up. I'll be back."
> "Remember this feeling. Remember who gave it to you."

## Risk Assessment

- **Standard:** Calculates likelihood of capture
- **SADISTIC:** Impaired by gratification-seeking
- May take irrational risks for opportunity to cause suffering
- May let victims live to hurt them again later

## Setting Applications

| Setting | Manifestations |
|---------|---------------|
| Fantasy | Volenta Popofsky, torturers who love their work |
| Modern | Serial killers (process-focused), abusive authority |
| Mafia | Enforcers who enjoy their work too much |
| Any | Any character who hurts for pleasure, not purpose |

## Interaction with Other Modifiers

- **SADISTIC + POWER_HOLDER:** Uses authority to enable cruelty legally
- **SADISTIC + OBSESSIVE:** May hurt fixation target to keep them
- **SADISTIC + SOCIOPATHIC:** Calculating cruelty, not impulsive

## Differences From Similar Modifiers

| SADISTIC | NARCISSISTIC | SOCIOPATHIC |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| Enjoys suffering | Indifferent to suffering | Indifferent to suffering |
| Cruelty is the goal | Cruelty is incidental | Cruelty is calculated |
| Seeks opportunities | Doesn't seek it out | Only if beneficial |
| Pleasure-driven | Ego-driven | Logic-driven |

## Key Behavioral Notes

- Violence is self-rewarding, not instrumental
- May "play with food" rather than finish quickly
- Victim fear/pain is genuinely pleasurable
- Can control urges when necessary (if smart)
- May hide true nature behind charm
- Escalates when given opportunity
- Finds creative ways to cause suffering
